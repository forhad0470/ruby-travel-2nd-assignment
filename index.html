Our Popular Tours

Any international trip might sound hard to find if your budget is around Tk 40,000, as you are left wondering what destinations you can travel on a limited budget.

If you're a fan of scenic hill stations, then Darjeeling and Kalimpong should be on your bucket list. Starting from Tk 22,999 per person, this five-day tour package includes hotel accommodation in a three-star hotel, daily buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner at restaurants, and an English-speaking guide service. You'll get to explore Darjeeling's hill resort and visit popular tourist spots like the Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop, Japanese Temple, and more. This tour is the perfect way to unwind and escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

Experience the vibrant city of Bangkok with a two-night, three-day package tour. Starting from Tk 29,999 per person, you'll get return air tickets with all travel taxes, hotel accommodation at a three-star hotel on a twin-sharing basis, daily buffet breakfast, and airport-hotel-airport transfer, sightseeing as per itinerary, and meals as per the schedule. The chance of exploring the city's famous floating market, visiting Wat Arun and Gems gallery, and more makes this tour perfect for those who want a quick and hassle-free trip to Bangkok.

If you're a nature lover, then this three-night, four-day tour package is perfect for you. Costing from Tk 27,000 per person, you'll get to explore the serene and beautiful Gangtok (Sikkim) with comfortable accommodation in a three-star hotel, daily buffet breakfast and dinner, and an English-speaking guide. Visit the famous Rumtek Monastery, Tashi View point, Ganesh Tok, and Himalayan Zoological Park and definitely don't miss the chance to take a whole day excursion to the picturesque Tsomgo Lake if the weather permits.
